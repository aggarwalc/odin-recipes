7-Layer Dip

Description

7-layer dip is a festive and flavorful appetizer that features a tantalizing combination of seven distinct layers, each adding its own texture and taste. Typically, this crowd-pleaser starts with a base of creamy refried beans, followed by a layer of rich sour cream mixed with zesty taco seasoning. Next comes a smooth layer of guacamole, topped with a generous spread of shredded cheddar cheese. A layer of crisp, diced tomatoes adds freshness, while chopped black olives and sliced green onions provide a touch of briny and aromatic contrast. Served with crunchy tortilla chips for dipping, this vibrant, multi-layered dip offers a satisfying blend of flavors and textures that are perfect for any gathering.

Ingredients

  • Avacados
  • Fresh Cilantro
  • Salsa
  • Lime Juice
  • Garlic Salt and Pepper
  • Sour Cream
  • Taco Seasoning
  • Refried Beans
  • Tomatoes
  • Green Onions
  • Mexican Cheese Blend
  • Black Olives

Steps

  1. Gather the ingredients.
  2. Mash avocados in a medium bowl to desired consistency. Mix in cilantro, salsa, lime juice, garlic salt, and pepper. Set aside.
  3. Blend sour cream and taco seasoning in a small bowl until combined. Set aside.
  4. Spread refried beans in a 9x13-inch dish or on a large serving platter. Top with sour cream mixture; cover with guacamole. Top with tomatoes, green onions, Mexican-style cheese blend, and black olives.
