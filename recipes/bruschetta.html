Bruschetta

Description

Bruschetta is a delightful Italian appetizer consisting of grilled or toasted slices of bread, typically rubbed with garlic and drizzled with extra virgin olive oil. The bread is often topped with a fresh mixture of diced tomatoes, basil, and a splash of balsamic vinegar, seasoned with salt and pepper. This simple yet flavorful dish offers a harmonious blend of crunchy texture and vibrant, aromatic toppings, making it a perfect starter or light snack.Â 

Ingredients

  • French Bread
  • Extra-Virgin Olive Oil
  • Plum Tomatoes
  • Fresh Basil
  • Parmesan Cheese
  • Garlic Cloves
  • Balsamic Vinegar
  • Salt and Pepper

Steps

  1. Gather all ingredients. Preheat the oven to 400 degrees F (200 degrees C).
  2. Brush bread slices on both sides lightly with 1 tablespoon oil and place on large baking sheet. Toast bread until golden, about 5 to 10 minutes, turning halfway through.
  3. Toss together tomatoes, basil, Parmesan cheese, and garlic in a bowl.
  4. Mix in balsamic vinegar, 2 teaspoons olive oil, kosher salt, and pepper.
  5. Spoon tomato mixture onto toasted bread slices; Serve immediately and enjoy!
