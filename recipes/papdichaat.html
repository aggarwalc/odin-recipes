Dahi Papdi Chaat

Description

A popular North Indian chaat recipe known for its sweet, sour and spicy taste. It is a yogurt based chaat recipe made with deep-fried flat puri and chaat chutneys.

Ingredients

  • 13 papdi
  • 3 tablespoons potato
  • 3 tablespoons chickpea
  • 3 tablespoons onion
  • 5 tablespoons curd
  • 3 teaspoons green chutney
  • 3 teaspoons tamarind chutney
  • Pinch of chili powder
  • Pinch of cumin powder
  • Pinch of chaat masala
  • Pinch of salt
  • 1 tablespoon tomato
  • 3 tablespoons sev
  • 1 teaspoon coriander

Steps

  1. Set papdi in a serving plate. Top with 3 tablespoons of potato and 3 tablespoons of chickpeas.
  2. Sprinkle 2 tablespoon chopped onions on top and drizzle 3 tablespoons of curd over it.
  3. Add 2 teaspoons of green chutney and 2 teaspoons of tamarind chutney.
  4. Sprinkle chili powder, cumin powder, chaat masala, and salt. Once again add 1 tablespoon of onions and 1 tablespoon of tomatos.
  5. Add 1 tablespoon curd, 1 teaspoon green chutney, and 1 teaspoon tamarind chutney.
  6. Top with 3 tablespoons sev and garnish with 1 teaspoon of coriander.
